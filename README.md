## â“ FAQ

**Q: Isn't this just prompt engineering?**  
A: No! AGENT is a *framework for strategic thinking with AI*. Prompts are one component. The protocols, questioning frameworks, and session management are what make it powerful.

**Q: Why only 100 prompts? Others have 1000s!**  
A: Quality > quantity. 100 well-designed prompts for common scenarios beats 1000 generic ones. Plus, we teach you to write your own.

**Q: Do I need to use a specific AI (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)?**  
A: No! AGENT framework works with any AI. Prompts are AI-agnostic (adjust wording if needed).

**Q: What if AI doesn't follow the protocol?**  
A: Remind it! "Use integrity questioning framework - challenge this assumption." Most AI will adapt when you set expectations.

**Q: Can I customize prompts?**  
A: Absolutely! Our prompts are templates. Adapt to your domain, voice, needs.

**Q: How is this different from generic "prompt libraries"?**  
A: AGENT is a complete collaboration framework, not just prompts. It includes: questioning protocols, session management, strategic thinking tools, conversational modes. Prompts are just one piece.

**Q: Why no Premium tier?**  
A: Lite Tier is feature-complete for individuals. We didn't want to artificially gate features just to create a paid tier. Business tier adds genuine team valueenterprise features).
